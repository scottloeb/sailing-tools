DataTrellis

Your friendly CSV exploration tool

Welcome to DataTrellis

DataTrellis helps you make sense of your spreadsheet data without needing to be a programmer. Think of it as a bridge between familiar spreadsheets and more powerful data tools.

The 15-Minute Promise: Within your first 15 minutes, you'll upload your own data, explore it with ease, and start seeing patterns that were hidden in your spreadsheets.

DataTrellis runs entirely in your web browser - there's nothing to install, and your data never leaves your computer. This makes it perfect for exploring sensitive information safely.

Getting Started

Opening DataTrellis

  1. Locate the data-trellis.html file you downloaded
  2. Double-click it to open in your browser (Chrome or Edge work best)
  3. You'll see a welcome screen with a drop zone for your files

Looking at Your First Dataset

  1. Find a CSV file you'd like to explore (any spreadsheet exported as CSV will work)
  2. Drag and drop it onto the drop zone in DataTrellis
  3. Your data will appear as a table, ready for exploration

That's it! You're already using DataTrellis. Try clicking column headers to sort your data, or use the search field to find specific information.

Exploring Your Data

Ways to Interact With Your Data

Once your CSV is loaded, you can:

  • Sort any column: Click a column header once for ascending order, twice for descending
  • Filter your data: Type in the search box to show only matching records
  • Choose what you see: Select which columns to display
  • See quick statistics: View summaries like totals, averages, and counts

Working With Multiple Files

Need to compare different datasets? DataTrellis lets you work with multiple files at once:

  1. Click the "+" tab at the top of the screen
  2. Select another CSV file to load
  3. Switch between your datasets by clicking their tabs

Helpful tip: While DataTrellis can handle very large files, it works best with datasets under 1 million rows. For extremely large files, you might notice it takes a moment to load.

Understanding How DataTrellis Works

DataTrellis is built like blocks that work together - each piece does one job really well, which makes the whole system both powerful and easy to understand.

Think of it like an office where:

  • The front desk keeps track of where everything is (this is called the AppRegistry)
  • The message system helps different departments communicate (the EventBus)
  • The filing system keeps all data organized and accessible (the AppState)
  • The data processing team turns raw information into useful formats (the CsvProcessor)
  • The search team helps find exactly what you need (the FilterSystem)
  • The display team presents everything in an understandable way (the DomUI)

This block-by-block design has a huge benefit: you can learn about and modify one piece at a time without needing to understand everything at once.

As you get more comfortable with DataTrellis, you'll find that this building-block approach makes it easy to customize the tool for your specific needs. You can start with simple changes and gradually work your way up to more sophisticated modifications.

Your Learning Path

As you get more comfortable with DataTrellis, you'll be able to:

  1. Filter and reshape your data to focus on what's important to you
  2. Create visualizations that make patterns easier to see
  3. Build custom tools that answer your specific questions
  4. Add new features through the plugin system
  5. Share your discoveries with colleagues in useful formats

Each step builds naturally on the one before it, creating a friendly path from basic data viewing to more powerful analysis.

Remember, the goal isn't to become a professional programmer - the goal is to make your data work for you. Focus on solving your immediate needs, and your skills will grow organically.
